Teaching Philosophy

Siphokazi Janet Ralane
Accredited Assessor (Services SETA) • Code 8 Driver’s License

Languages: IsiXhosa (Excellent), English (Excellent), IsiZulu (Good), Afrikaans (Fair)

My philosophy is grounded in student-centered learning, constructive alignment, and authentic assessment. I aim to cultivate independent, reflective graduates who can apply management concepts in real contexts.

  • Design learning experiences that link theory to practice (case studies from South African enterprises).
  • Create inclusive classrooms where language diversity and varied backgrounds are valued.
  • Emphasize feedback loops—feedforward guidance that improves subsequent performance.
“Tell me and I forget. Teach me and I remember. Involve me and I learn.”
